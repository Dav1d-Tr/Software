What does a developer do?

Software or Systems Developers work for firms and companies dedicated to the field of Computing. They are in charge of developing the pillars of the operating systems that are created by the Programmers and of testing the code of new programs to guarantee their efficiency. They also carry out quality tests on new projects before they are launched.

Knowledge

The main role is the creation and adaptation of computer programs, although this is obviously a somewhat simplistic description. Its scope covers a number of applications, such as programs, processes, networks, version upgrades, patches, migrations, DevOps, and testing.
