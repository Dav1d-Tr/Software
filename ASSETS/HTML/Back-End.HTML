What is a Back-End Developer?

A backend developer is the person in charge of the development of a web, app or program, he is in charge of the "back end", the back; that is to say, of working with the server. His functions are to design, review and repair everything for its correct operation.

The backend refers to all those elements of the website, application or program that are inaccessible to users. Managing the backend means creating the logic, connecting it to the server and managing the data or databases.
